About this tool.

This tool calculates the Wolf Number, an indicator of Solar activity based on the number and grouping of sunspots.

Double-click to add a new group, click-and-drag to move groups around.

Use the plus and minus buttons to add or remove sunspots from each group and calculate the Wolf Number.
